Bieber was discovered on YouTube in 2007, and since then his career has sky-rocketed. After being discovered, Bieber moved to Atlanta, GA and began his music career. Now, Bieber has released 8 albums and gone on several world tours. Bieber's fanbase, known as "beliebers," consists of mostly teenaged girls.
